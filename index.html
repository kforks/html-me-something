Frogs From My Research

Why Frogs?

Before I was a programmer, I was a field biologist who studied "Herpetology." Herpetology is the study of reptiles and amphibians. My dad co-wrote the Field Guide to Texas Snakes, so I grew up with a deep appreciation for the natural world! I went on to study Wildlife Biology in college and conducted research in Texas, Costa Rica and the Caribbean islands. I primrily studied fungal disease and the use of artificial refugia by tree frogs. So, I chose to use my webpage to share some of the cool frogs I found on my adventures with you!
